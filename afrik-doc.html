Afrik'Up Programs

Introduction

Afrik'Up is dedicated to unlocking the potential of African women as leaders, entrepreneurs, and change-makers. Our programs are designed to support women in developing their skills, achieving financial independence, and creating opportunities through a strong and structured network.

SEFA Program

SEFA: The University of Entrepreneurship and Personal Success is a structured pathway that guides women through each stage of their transformation, from self-discovery to leadership.

  • BAC SEFA – GENESIS: Self-Discovery and Initiation (2 months)
  • BTS SEFA – IMPULSION: Launching a Project (3 months)
  • LICENCE SEFA – ASCENSION: Building a Sustainable Business (6 months)
  • MASTER SEFA – APOTHEOSIS: Becoming a Leader and Industry Influencer (1 year)

EmpowerHer

EmpowerHer is a community-driven initiative that supports women through annual cycles focused on leadership, economic empowerment, well-being, and gender equality.

Starting a Business in 2025 with 100,000 FCFA

This innovative program allows participants to launch a profitable project with a minimal initial investment of 100,000 FCFA, benefiting from six months of coaching and support.

Partnership Opportunities

Partner with Afrik'Up to actively contribute to the empowerment of African women, demonstrate your social impact, join a dynamic network of African leaders and entrepreneurs, and be part of an ambitious project with significant growth and impact potential.
